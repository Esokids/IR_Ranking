Get your thinking caps ready because we're gonna take this snake game in a new level. Snake and Blocks is a new take on the classic game snakes. In this game, your mission is to go as far as you can. You need to collect all those balls which will add up in your snake's length. You need your snake to be longer so you can go through the blocks. The number of balls in your snake should be greater than the number indicated on the block because after passing though it your snake's ball will be deducted by the number indicated on the block. It's highly addictive, enjoyable and mind enhancing!
