In Episode 2 of The Miller Estate, players take control of Ophelia as she do her investigation inside the Miller Estate. While Prescott explodes outside the estate and Dr.MacDermoth inside the study, Ophelia, with her parapsychic power, has a strange feeling of someone waiting for her. As night comes, she can sense a dark atmosphere is surrounding the estate. She decides that she has to do something about it as she has “no other choice”.
