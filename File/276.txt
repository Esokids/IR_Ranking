NexGen Tennis is the best browser based tennis game that will truly give you the feel of playing tennis in a real gaming console. Customize your player, choose their outfit and rockets. Play in 2 different modes World Tour and Exhibition. In World Tour, you'll be able to choose different places from around the world with different category and court surfaces. While in Exhibition you'll play in 4 different courts which are the clay, grass, hard and carpet. Defeat all your opponents and be the first one to have your name in the leaderboard!
