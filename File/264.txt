Tired of the same TicTacToe game? Well if you want a greater challenge and a new way of doing the regular then this game is for you. The Ultimate TicTacToe will give you not just the regular 3x3 but also 5x5 and 7x7. In these new grids your objective is to make 4 matches. And the best part of this game is that you can play it with your friends! Compete with them and be in the high score list!
