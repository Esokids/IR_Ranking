(CNN)"Maleficent: Mistress of Evil" we have been waiting on you.

Disney has released the teaser trailer for the sequel to the 2014 film loosely based on the villainess from "Sleeping Beauty."
Angelina Jolie's Maleficent looks to be locking horns with Queen Ingrith, played by Michelle Pfeiffer, this time around.
Judging by one scene in the teaser, Queen Ingrith and King John (played by Robert Lindsay) are not on Maleficent's good side.
