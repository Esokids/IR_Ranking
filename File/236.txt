Ready...Set...Hide! Welcome to Prop Buster, a fun 3D shooting game. Be the Hunter that search and kill those Props or be one of the Props that shape shift and hide away from the Hunters and tease them by playing silly taunts. If you're a Hunter the only way to win this game is to kill all the Props before the time runs out. But when you're a Prop, the only way to win the game is to survive and not be killed until the time runs out. It's a fun hide and seek game that will definitely be enjoyed by everyone! So prepare yourself and experience the fun in playing this game, Prop Busters, a different kind of search and destroy or hide and survive!
